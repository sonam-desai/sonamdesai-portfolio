Apple_Stock_Price_Prediction

Apple’s stock prices are subject to fluctuate as it is based on many factors like social and economic factors, change in global economy, industry trends etc. The motive to select this company is because the company is publicly traded with strong financial performance and a strong brand recognition around the world. The data set is for stock price of Apple Inc. and our goal will be to build the model by plotting the time series graph with its respective ACF & PACF to analyse if the model is stationary or not. If not, the we will further perform its log and differenced series and will plot the time-series graph with ACF & PACF, EACF & ADF test to check if the curve shows the stationarity. If the model seems to be stationary then we will identify which model we will choose and perform parameter estimation by identifying significant and non-significant coefficients and will check if the model is adequate. If the model seems to be adequate then we’ll do the forecasting else will repeat the process of identifying which model to choose.
