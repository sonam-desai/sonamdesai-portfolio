Over the past 3 years crimes in LA have rapidly increased. Crimes are considered as difficult to predict because people could commit crime at random, however it would help reduce crime rate and future loss. Our motive is to find out insightful data extraction, analysis, and the discovery of patterns, trends, and possible correlations within the crime data. For the purpose of forecasting and predicting the top 5 criminal activities in the city, we are utilizing multiple machine learning models. Our ultimate goal in undertaking this project is to make Los Angeles a more secure and safe place.
